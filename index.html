• About me

    I graduated with a Degree in Robotics & Mechatronics Engineering and have been working in Hospitality for a while before I decided, with the encouragement of my loved ones, to make the bold decision to take the risk and pursue my passion in Web Development.

    I signed up with General Assembly Melbourne and was well on my way to becoming a Full Stack Web Developer. During the intensive Web Development Course with GA, we were taught the up-to-date technologies in the Software Development field for the internet. The learning is fast paced and very detailed.

    My strength is definitely my personality. I have a curious mind and treat learning as a life long journey. I am willing to take action and follow through. Above all, I have the tenacity and perseverance to rise from my failures. I am well versed in both ends of Web Development, although my strengths are prominent in the Back End (Server Side). I love Ruby on Rails and JavaScript. I am an awesome Scrum Master who enjoys Pair Programming and is capable of pulling my own weight in group projects.

    I bake occasionally to bring people together, sing out loud in the shower to relief stress and enjoy meeting new people. I am determined to be ONE OF THE MOST SUCCESSFUL PERSON in the Tech Industry through Web Development someday as I owe it to my loved ones and myself to see this through.
